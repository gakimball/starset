Orbit Gum

When: 2018

What: Sound Design, Mix, Edit

The sounds of a tiny process through a magnifying glass.

What does a microscopic wire basket sound like? The reality...not much. But we weren’t tasked with reality on this one. This piece focuses on separating the production environment from the tiny tasks at hand and the score and sound were to play crucial roles in this separation.
